MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2026, by and between Alpha Legal Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Provider") and Northwind Retail LLC ("Client").

1. Scope of Services
Provider shall deliver document review, contract abstraction, and compliance reporting services as defined in Statement of Work SOW-001.

2. Term and Termination
The initial term is twelve (12) months from the Effective Date. Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice.

3. Confidentiality
Each party shall maintain strict confidentiality of non-public business, legal, and technical information. Confidential information may be disclosed only to employees or contractors with a need to know and subject to equivalent confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Protection
Provider shall implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect Client data. Provider shall notify Client of any confirmed data breach without undue delay and no later than seventy-two (72) hours after confirmation.

5. Limitation of Liability
Except for willful misconduct, fraud, or confidentiality violations, each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed fees paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.
